## Privacy Policy for codesennin.com

Last Updated: January 15, 2025

### Overview

This privacy policy exists because I believe in transparency about how this site operates.

**codesennin.com is a static website** hosted on GitHub Pages. I don't sell any data and only collect minimal information necessary for site functionality (comments and analytics).

### What Information Is Collected

#### Google Analytics

This site uses Google Analytics to understand how visitors interact with the content.

You can opt out of Google Analytics by:
- Declining cookies when you first visit (via the cookie banner)

#### Comments

If you post a comment on this blog:
- Only your **display name** and **message** are required
- Your display name is sent to [Robohash.org](https://robohash.org) to generate a unique avatar image
- You can optionally provide a URL (website, social profile, etc.)
- A "Remember Me" feature stores your name and URL locally in your browser for convenience
- All comment data (name, message, and optional URL) is publicly visible once approved

#### Cookies

This site uses cookies for:
- **Google Analytics:** Tracking site usage (requires your consent)
- **reCAPTCHA:** Spam protection on comment forms (Google service)
- **Local Storage:** Remembering your name and URL if you use "Remember Me" on comments

### What I Don't Do

- I **do NOT** require user registration
- I **do NOT** collect email addresses
- I **do NOT** sell, trade, or transfer visitor information to third parties
- I **do NOT** collect sensitive personal data
- I **do NOT** send marketing emails
- I **do NOT** share data except in aggregate form for statistics

### Third-Party Services

This site uses these third-party services that have their own privacy policies:

- **Google Analytics** - [Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy)
- **Google reCAPTCHA** - [Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy)
- **Robohash** (avatar generation) - [https://robohash.org](https://robohash.org)
- **GitHub Pages** (hosting) - [Privacy Statement](https://docs.github.com/en/site-policy/privacy-policies/github-privacy-statement)

### Your Rights

You have the right to:
- Decline cookies (via the cookie consent banner)
- Request deletion of your comments (contact me via the site)
- Know what data is collected (as outlined above)
